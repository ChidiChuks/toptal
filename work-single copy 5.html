Plant Identifier App

Role UI/UX Design
Team Freelancer
Company PlantAv

Overview

Identify plants, flowers, cacti, succulents and mushrooms in seconds with the click of a button on your mobile device.

With the concept of creating the biggest scientific database of plants and also striving to make it fun and easy for the young generations to interact with nature and grow up in the spirit of protecting this amazing planet we live on.

I was the main designer working on this project in March 2020.

The Problem

Giving users the ability to save their favorite plants in real-time meant making fundamental changes to the way artificial intelligence have worked since PlantAv App started. Goals

- User : Make plant snap naming and identification whenever they want

- Business : Increase engagement and retention of users
